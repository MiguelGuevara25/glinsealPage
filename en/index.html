HOW DOES RFID TECHNOLOGY WORK?

Our RFID solution uses tags and seals with different qualities to suit various needs. It uses antennas and readers in fixed gantries for automated processes, in addition to RFID/UHF handheld readers.

As the RFID tags or seals are reached by the coverage of the antennas, they report their identification code (unique and impossible to clone), in addition our RFID seals can report integrity (manipulated / not manipulated) and condition (open / closed).

WHAT IS A HIGH SECURITY RFID 3-STATE SEAL?

It is a device that complies with the highest security standards worldwide, capable of providing RFID IDENTIFICATION information, if its integrity is HANDLED NOT or HANDLED plus the condition of use OPEN or CLOSED.
